Common Reasons Cars Don't Run

Dead Battery

Battery won't hold a charge or is completely dead. We still buy these cars for parts and scrap value.

Engine Problems

Engine won't start, seized engine, or major mechanical failure. We buy cars with any engine issues.

Fuel System Issues

Fuel pump failure, clogged fuel lines, or other fuel system problems that prevent starting.

Electrical Problems

Wiring issues, alternator failure, or other electrical problems that prevent the car from running.

Transmission Failure

Transmission won't engage, slipping gears, or complete transmission failure.

Unknown Issues

Car just won't start and you don't know why. We'll buy it regardless of the unknown problem.

What Determines Value for Non-Running Cars?

Weight & Size

Larger, heavier cars typically have more scrap metal value. SUVs and trucks often fetch higher prices.

Parts Value

Popular makes and models have higher parts demand, increasing the overall value of the vehicle.

Year & Model

Newer cars and popular models typically have higher scrap and parts values.

Market Prices

Current scrap metal prices and market demand affect the value we can offer for your car.
