Frequently Asked Questions

How long does the entire process take?

From initial contact to payment, the entire process typically takes 1-3 days, depending on your schedule and our availability.

Do I need to prepare anything for the inspection?

Just have your car title and any service records available. We'll handle the rest during the inspection.

What if I'm not happy with the final offer?

No problem! There's no obligation to sell. You can walk away at any time with no fees or charges.

Do you buy cars that don't run?

Yes! We buy cars in any condition - running, damaged, or non-running. We'll factor the condition into our offer.

What paperwork do I need?

You'll need the car title and a valid ID. We'll handle all the transfer paperwork for you.

Is the inspection really free?

Absolutely! Our inspection is completely free with no hidden fees or charges.
