Types of Damage We Accept

Accident Damage

Front-end, rear-end, side impact, or total loss accidents. We buy cars with any level of collision damage.

  • Front-end collisions
  • Rear-end accidents
  • Side impact damage
  • Total loss vehicles

Mechanical Issues

Engine problems, transmission failure, or other mechanical issues that make the car undrivable.

  • Engine failure
  • Transmission problems
  • Electrical issues
  • Brake system failure

Flood Damage

Water damage from floods, hurricanes, or other water-related incidents.

  • Flood damage
  • Hurricane damage
  • Water intrusion
  • Mold damage

Fire Damage

Vehicles damaged by fire, whether from accidents or other causes.

  • Engine fire
  • Electrical fire
  • Accident fire
  • Smoke damage

Weather Damage

Hail damage, storm damage, or other weather-related vehicle damage.

  • Hail damage
  • Storm damage
  • Tree damage
  • Wind damage

Other Damage

Any other type of damage that affects your vehicle's value or drivability.

  • Vandalism
  • Theft recovery
  • Rust damage
  • Frame damage

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you buy cars that don't run?

Yes! We buy damaged cars regardless of whether they run or not. We can tow non-running vehicles for free.

How much can I get for my damaged car?

Prices depend on the type and extent of damage, but we typically offer 20-60% of the car's pre-damage value.

What if my car is a total loss?

We specialize in total loss vehicles and can often offer better prices than insurance companies for salvage value.

Do I need to fix the car before selling?

No! We buy cars in their current damaged condition. Don't waste money on repairs - sell as-is.
